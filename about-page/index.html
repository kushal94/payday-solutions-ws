About Us

Founder & CEO

Gurdeep Singh

I’m Hardeep, a payroll expert with a focus on supporting small and medium-sized businesses.

Over the years, I’ve assisted numerous businesses with payroll setup, training, data corrections, and resolving queries. I founded Payday Solutions Payroll after noticing the widespread confusion and errors many small business owners face when it comes to payroll. Given that payroll is often one of the largest expenses for a business, getting it wrong can lead to significant penalties.

I understand that as a small business owner, your time is limited, and dealing with administrative tasks during work hours isn’t always feasible. You didn’t start your business to be bogged down by the Holidays Act or the stress of making payroll mistakes. That’s where I step in. I work around your schedule, ensuring your payroll is set up correctly from the beginning. I also provide training on payroll software, helping you get the most out of it and use it accurately. Since payroll is not something you can simply “set and forget,” I offer payroll audit services to keep your business compliant.

I’m passionate about payroll legislation and committed to getting it right every time.
