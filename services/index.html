Services

Payroll Onboarding

Our Payroll Onboarding service is designed to simplify the transition from one payroll system to another, reducing the stress associated with data transfer and ensuring historical accuracy. We handle the setup for both new and existing employees, review and adjust leave balances prior to transfer, and enter the last 52 weeks of earnings to ensure a smooth transition. Pricing starts at $99+GST for up to 5 employees, with an additional $20+GST per employee thereafter.

Payroll Software Training

We provide tailored Payroll Software Training for XERO, SMARTLY, and iPayroll, delivered through Google Meets, Team Viewer, or in person (only in Auckland). This training is customized to fit your learning needs, equipping you with the skills necessary to efficiently use your payroll software. Sessions are priced from $125+GST for approximately 1.5 hours, ensuring a comprehensive understanding of your chosen platform.

Payroll Auditing

Our Payroll Audit service is designed to identify and resolve configuration issues, ensuring that all employees are set up correctly based on their specific working arrangements and pay structures. We also review leave balances to confirm their accuracy according to current agreements. Following the audit, we provide detailed findings and actionable recommendations. Pricing begins at $300+GST for up to 5 employees, with an additional $35+GST per employee thereafter.

Managed Payroll Services

We offer Managed Payroll Services tailored to your unique requirements, including pay complexity and the number of employees. Our custom solutions are designed to fit your specific needs, ensuring efficient and accurate payroll management. For more details and to discuss how we can support your payroll needs, please contact us directly.
